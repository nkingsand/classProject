Beau Daniels Band Fan Site!

BIO

Beau Daniels Band is a bootknockin' Texas Country Band based out of New Braunfels, TX.

Known for their original music and cover songs, you can enjoy listening to their performances throughout central Texas or book them to play for a special event!

Members:

  1. Tommy Harrison, Guitarist
  2. Beau Daniels, Guitarist, Songwriter, Vocalist
  3. Aaron Woods, Drummer
  4. Travis Street, Bassist, Vocalist
